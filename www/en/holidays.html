Holidays and school closure

The preschool is closed on all statutory holidays. In addition, there is a two-week Christmas Break and a one-week Spring Break. Our Christmas Break coincides with the Vancouver School Board’s Winter Break. Our Spring Break coincides with the second week of the Vancouver School Board’s two-week Spring Break. The Secretary will send out a list of school closure dates for the current year shortly after the preschool opens in September.

Pomme d’Api will be closed on:

  • Thanksgiving
  • Remembrance Day
  • Christmas (two-week vacation)
  • Family Day
  • Spring Break (one-week vacation)
  • Good Friday
  • Easter Monday
  • Victoria Day
